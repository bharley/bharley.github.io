If you're in love with the stupid doge meme as much as I am, you're going to watch to be greeted by it every time you launch your shell. Right? Well I know I do.

  1. Install the doge python script
  2. Add doge to the end of your .bash_rc/.bash_profile

Now you have the internet's favorite meme annoying you every time you try to get anything done.

Bonus: He's even in the holiday spirit.
