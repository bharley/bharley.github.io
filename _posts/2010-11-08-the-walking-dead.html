As a zombie fanatic, a show called The Walking Dead would obviously pique my interest. The Walking Dead, a television series on AMC about zombies and a post-apocalyptic world, is the first time—to my knowledge—that the zombie genre has been made for television.

After discovering it yesterday evening, I immediately watched the first (and only) two episodes. Although I never read the comics this show is based on, I'm enjoying what's been shown so far.

The show itself is your standard zombie affair, and it doesn't deviate too much from the lore laid down by heavy-hitters like George A. Ramero. However, that's the way I like my zombies.

If you're a fan of zombies, I highly suggest you check it out.
